Environment Canada NPRI default treatment categories

NPRI provides values for wastewater, including PFAS not analyzed by EPA 1633A, based on three sets of treatment parameters. All combinations of these default liquid & solid types have been pre-calculated and the closest matching to your facility should be selected using the table of content links to the right.
